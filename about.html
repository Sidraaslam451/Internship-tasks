About us

Built by engineers who've been paged at 3am.

Canopy Systems started because we were tired of watching good businesses get let down by fragile software. So we built the company we wished we could have hired.

Our story

Founded in 2017

Canopy Systems was founded by a small team of infrastructure engineers who kept getting called in to fix the same problem: software that worked in a demo but fell apart under real customers. Nine years later, we've grown into a full-service partner for cloud infrastructure and custom software, but the original goal hasn't changed — build things that hold up.

Our approach

Boring in the best way

We're not chasing the newest framework. We pick technology that's proven, document everything, and design systems so the next engineer — whether that's us in a year or someone on your team — can understand what's happening and why.

What we value

Three things we don't compromise on

Transparency

You get access to the same dashboards, logs, and documentation we use internally. No hidden systems.

Reliability

We measure ourselves on uptime and response time, not just delivery dates.

Plain communication

We explain technical decisions in terms that make sense to whoever's asking — no jargon walls.
